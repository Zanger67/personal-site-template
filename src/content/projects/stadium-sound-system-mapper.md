## Overview

Bobby Dodd Stadium is loud — but not evenly. We mapped every speaker to find
the dead zones swallowing the crowd's chants.

Used acoustic modeling to find dead zones in the stadium PA system and proposed
speaker repositioning for maximum chant clarity.

### Findings

- Three under-served sections in the upper deck.
- A proposed re-aim of six speakers for even coverage.
