## Overview

Each year the Ramblin' Wreck gets a full once-over before it leads the team
onto the field. This project coordinated that effort — from sourcing
period-correct parts to scheduling volunteer wrench time.

### What we did

- Catalogued every component due for service and tracked it on a shared board.
- Recruited and scheduled volunteer mechanics across the semester.
- Documented the restoration with before/after photos for the archive.

It pairs nicely with the rest of the [campus projects](/projects).
