## Overview

*Ramblin' Wreck from Georgia Tech* has been remixed more times than anyone can
count. This archive tries to count anyway.

### What's inside

- Every known arrangement, from marching band to lo-fi.
- Source credits and recording dates where we could find them.
- A submission form so the collection keeps growing.
