## Overview

The mascot lounge — affectionately "the Hive" — hadn't been touched in years.
We reimagined it as part museum, part hangout.

### Highlights

- A historical photo wall spanning decades of Buzz.
- Rotating memorabilia displays with proper lighting.
- A cozy reading nook stocked with old yearbooks.
